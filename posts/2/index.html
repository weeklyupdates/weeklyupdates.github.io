2 - The Final Placeholder

Hello again. This is the final placeholder entry.

I don’t really like using markdown, but why not.

It is nice to write entries in VIM. I wonder if it looks terrible. I am typing more to see how a large paragraph looks. Sometimes I do words per minute speedtests online. I can type 90 words per minute. I do it in a strange way; it is not the normal home row method. I did not take a keyboarding class in high school, because I figured other classes would be more worth my time. Little did I know, virtually none of the classes in my high school were worth my time.

And with that, this final temporary update comes to a close. I hope you, who were lucky enough to have seen it, enjoyed it.
